Welcome to College Advice Counseling!

A college application resource for high schoolers interested in higher education

CAC Purpose

We made this app to empower high schoolers by spreading college-related opportunities and resources. We were inspired by our own struggles with finding college advice and resources. We believe that having this collection of resources will encourage more high schoolers to consider higher education.

What this site includes:

College advice that current college students or working adults wish they knew in high school when applying for college and information about UNC system colleges and universities based on your preferences!
